Privacy Policy

Effective May 1, 2026 · Elevate Women's Conference 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what the Elevate Women's Conference 2026 photo booth kiosk (the "Kiosk") collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. We've kept it short on purpose. If anything is unclear, email hello@powerwyze.com.

The short version: The Kiosk only collects what it needs to make and send you your souvenir portrait — your name, optionally your phone or email, and a single photo of your face. We don't sell your data, we don't use your face to train AI, and you can ask us to delete everything at any time.

1. The data we collect

DataRequired?Why we collect it
First name & last name Required to start To personalize your souvenir and address your delivery message.
Phone number Optional To text you a link to your portrait.
Email address Optional To email you a link to your portrait.
A photo of your face Required Sent to our AI partner to generate the stylized portrait.

2. How consent is captured

Consent is given in person, at the physical Kiosk, by tapping "I agree" before entering any contact information. We do not collect data from anyone who does not approach the Kiosk and complete that step. There is no tracking, cookie banner, or background analytics on this site beyond what's strictly needed to render these pages.

3. How we use your data

  • To generate your portrait. Your photo is sent over an encrypted connection to our AI image provider (Google Gemini "Nano Banana") for one rendering pass and is not retained by them after.
  • To deliver your portrait. If you provide a phone number, we send a single MMS via our SMS provider (Twilio, with ClickSend as a backup). If you provide an email, we email it via our email provider.
  • To host your portrait so the link works. The finished image is stored on Supabase (our hosting provider) for up to 30 days, then automatically deleted.

4. What we do not do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not share your data with advertisers or third-party marketers.
  • We do not use your face image to train AI models.
  • We do not text or email you again unless you've separately opted in elsewhere (e.g. signing up for the conference newsletter).

5. Retention

WhatWhereHow long
Your captured face photoSent to AI provider, then discardedDiscarded after rendering (minutes)

Your name, phone, emailSMS / email provider message logUp to 30 days

6. SMS-specific terms (US carriers)

By providing your phone number at the Kiosk, you consent to receive a single MMS message containing your souvenir image from Elevate Women's Conference. Message frequency: one per opt-in. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe. Reply HELP for help.

7. Your choices and rights

You can:

  • Use the QR code on the result screen to save your image without giving us a phone or email.
  • Reply STOP to any of our texts to opt out.
  • Email hello@powerwyze.com to ask us to delete your photo, contact info, or both.

If you live in California, the EU, or another jurisdiction with specific privacy laws (CCPA, GDPR, etc.), you have additional rights including the right to know what we hold about you and the right to deletion. Email us and we'll honor those requests.

8. Service providers we use

  • Google Gemini ("Nano Banana") — AI image generation
  • Supabase — image hosting
  • Twilio / ClickSend — SMS/MMS delivery
  • Vercel — kiosk web hosting

Each of these providers has its own privacy practices governing data we send them.

9. Security

Connections to the Kiosk and to our service providers use HTTPS/TLS. The Kiosk runs on a private device at the conference venue and does not display your data to other guests.

10. Children

The Kiosk is intended for adult conference attendees. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13.

11. Changes

If this Privacy Policy changes before or during the event, we'll post the updated version at this URL with a new effective date.

12. Contact

Privacy questions or deletion requests: hello@powerwyze.com
