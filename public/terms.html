1. About this experience

The Elevate Women's Conference Photo Booth (the "Kiosk") is an on-site interactive experience operated by the Elevate Women's Conference 2026 ("Elevate," "we," "us"). The Kiosk lets attendees opt in to capture a photo, receive an AI-generated souvenir portrait themed around Capri, Italy, and have that portrait delivered to their phone or email.

By tapping "I agree" at the consent screen and continuing through the Kiosk flow, you accept these Terms and the Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not continue past the consent screen.

2. What we collect

The Kiosk only collects the minimum information needed to deliver your souvenir:

  • First name and last name — used to personalize your message and label your image file.
  • Phone number — used to text you a link to your image (optional).
  • Email address — used to email you a link to your image (optional).
  • A still photo of your face — captured locally on the Kiosk and sent to our AI image partner to render the souvenir portrait.

You may choose to provide phone, email, both, or neither. If you provide neither, you can still scan an on-screen QR code to download your image without giving us contact details.

3. How we use it

  • To generate your souvenir portrait.
  • To deliver the portrait to you via text message and/or email.
  • To send you a single follow-up message about the conference if you have separately opted in elsewhere.

We do not sell your information, share it with advertisers, or use your face data to train AI models.

Single message commitment: By providing your phone number at the Kiosk, you consent to receive one (1) MMS message containing your souvenir image. Reply STOP at any time to opt out of any further messages. Reply HELP for help. Message and data rates may apply.

4. Use of the AI portrait

The souvenir portrait is provided to you for personal, non-commercial use. You're welcome to share it on your personal social media. Elevate may showcase a curated selection of portraits at the conference recap (faces blurred or with separate permission). You retain rights to your underlying likeness.

5. Photo retention

The captured photo of your face is sent to our AI partner only for the duration of one render and is not retained by them after generation. The finished souvenir image is stored on our hosting provider for up to 30 days so the link in your text or email keeps working, then it's automatically deleted.
